Tic Tac Toe

Game Rules

  1. Gameplay:
    • The game begins with Player 1 taking the first turn.
    • Players take turns placing their respective symbols (X or O) on an empty cell of the grid.
    • Each player can only place their symbol in an unoccupied cell.
  2. Objective:
    • The goal is to be the first player to form a horizontal, vertical, or diagonal line of three of their own symbols.
    • The line can be formed in any direction: horizontally, vertically, or diagonally.
  3. Winning conditions:
    • If a player successfully fills any horizontal, vertical, or diagonal line with their symbols, they win the game.
    • If all cells are occupied and no player has formed a winning line, the game ends in a draw.
  4. End of game:
    • The game ends when one player forms a winning line or when the game reaches a draw.
    • After the game ends, players may choose to restart and play again.
